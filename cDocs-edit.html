Editing Grocery Items

A grocery item's "Edit" submenu button is accessed in the pantry view by sliding the grocery item name to the left.

Pressing the "Edit" button in the pantry view opens that grocery item's editing dialog window:

The item "Item Name" box provides you with a convenient way to correct any spelling errors or add a special character such as the dash "-".

Similarly, the "Department" box allows you to change an item's location in the store, or if you have added a separate store to your department list then you could also change the item's store in its entirety.

"Shelf Life" determines how many days a grocery item lasts before it must be purchased again and is reflected by the item's dot color.  The elapsed days before a repurchase may be adjusted using the "<<" or "<" buttons to decrease shelf life by increments of 30 days or 1 day respectfully. Similarly shelf life may be increased by touching the ">" or ">>" buttons.  The sensitivity bar reflects to what degree purchases should effect adjustments to shelf life: the higher the sensitivity, the more often a purchase will change shelf life.  For example: if you have an item you only purchase once a month then, set the shelf life to 30 days and slide the sensitivity bar all the way to the left. In this way if you repurchase the item in advance due to a sale or special, the item's shelf life will not be changed to any degree.
