Pantry - All Groceries

Pressing the "x" button switched you from your shopping list to your pantry view.  All your groceries are listed in alphabetical order with groceries already included on your shopping list shown as grayed.  To add or remove an item from your shopping list, simply swipe the item right.  If the item is already on your shopping list, the item will be returned to the pantry without any update to average shelf life.  Just as in your shopping list, an item's shelf life is indicated by colored dots, in addition to red and yellow dots:

The green dots next to the "Apples"  and "Peanut Butter" items in the example view above suggests that these items are currently on hand at home.

A gray dot means that the item is included on your shopping list or that the item has not been purchased enough times (two) to determine an average shelf life.

Included in this view are three buttons and two submenu buttons:

The "x" button returns you to your Shopping List view.

The "+" button opens the Add Grocery view allowing you to add new groceries to your grocery list.

Touching the "<" button slides open the Settings view.

Pressing the "Edit" button in the pantry view opens that grocery item's editing dialog window.

Pressing the "Delete" button in the pantry view opens the grocery delete warning asking you to confirm the permanent removal of the grocery item.
