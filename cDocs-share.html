Sharing Your Grocery List

Touching the ">" button will return you to the pantry view.

In order to share your grocery list across devices, Grocery Gopher makes use of cloud storage associated with your Google account which is exclusively set aside for Android applications.  Visit the Google Drive page for more information about this feature which provides everyone with 15GB of free storage:
  • Toggling the Google Drive switch to "On" opens the Google Drive Access authorization dialog.
  • Once enabled, Google Drive is disabled by toggling the switch to the "Off" position.

Synchronization with the cloud is accomplished either manually and/or automatically on a preset daily, weekly or monthly schedule:
  • Toggling the Auto Synchronize switch to "On" opens the Sharing and Synchronizing dialog.
  • Manual synchronization only is enabled by toggling the Auto Synchronize switch to the "Off" position.
Touching the "Upload/Download" button opens the Google Drive Sync dialog.  This allows you to choose whether to upload the grocery list data on your phone to replace the cloud data, or to download the grocery list data already stored in the cloud to replace the grocery list data on your phone.  This is unlike the  button in the Shopping List view which uploads or downloads automatically without letting you choose which specific data file.
