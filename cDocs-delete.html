Deleting Groceries

A grocery item's "Delete" submenu button is accessed in the pantry view by sliding the grocery item name to the left.

Pressing the "Delete" button in the pantry view opens the grocery delete warning asking you to confirm the permanent removal of the grocery item:

The "Yes" button removes the item however, it doesn't remove the item from any backup or from the cloud file until you create a new backup or synchronize your grocery list.

Touching the "No" button cancels the selection and the item remains as one of your groceries.
