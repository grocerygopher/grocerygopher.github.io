General Settings

Touching the "<" button opened the settings view and
Touching the ">" button will return you to the pantry view.

Individual settings are turned "Off" or
"On" by toggle switches.

 Under the "General Settings" subheading, your options include:
  • Hiding groceries with a current shelf life greater than 30 days from displaying in the pantry view. Groceries will not appear on the pantry list until the item is within 30 days of purchase.
  • Enabling special formatting characters in grocery item names.  Currently only the dash, "-" is a recognized special character.  When the dash is used as a special character, the department name is hidden and replaced by the text after the dash in the grocery item name.  This is useful if you want to include a specific brand in the name such as: "Coffee - Folgers" in "Dry Goods" would appear as "Coffee" with "Folgers" underneath where the department name is usually listed; the "Dry Goods" department would be hidden from view.  However, the item would still appear with other items in "Dry Goods" on your shopping list; this feature does not replace the department.
  • Locking Grocery Gopher into portrait mode only.  This does not effect any other app on your phone, it locks only the Grocery Gopher app.
